Hello, I'm Monica.
My name is Monica Wilcox. I am a third year at the University of Cincinnati studying Fashion Design in the college of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP). I have worked as a design intern at Abercrombie & Fitch as a part of the Hollister Guy's Tops team and for Josie Natori's RTW team. As a designer I aspire to create innovative designs while focusing on the quality and wearability of the garments.

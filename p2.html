Ink Blot
A study of print, structure, and trying to find the extraordinary
Textile donations from Ikea allowed for an exploration beyond the fabrics intended use. Ever since Leonardo da Vinci urged artists to search for inspiration in the dirt on walls or the streaked patterns in stones, they have found that the accidental blot, the chance mark, or the naturally occurring stain can be starting point for some extraordinary art. This is the study of print, structure and trying to find that extraordinary.
